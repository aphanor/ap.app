International Recruitment Agency for
Private Households and Family Offices

Based in London and Zürich, Luxury Assets is a boutique recruitment agency, providing bespoke staffing solutions for domestic households and family offices.

We offer an elite recruitment service that is discreet, reliable and personal to clients and candidates alike with strong values and guidelines in place.

Our international clients are discerning, ultra high-net-worth individuals, seeking to staff their prestigious homes, chalets, multiple estates, luxury yachts and aircrafts.

We also recruit staff for family offices, wealth management companies and embassies. Our clients are based in Europe, Switzerland, the UK, Germany, the Balearic Islands, the Middle East, and the USA.

Our high-calibre candidates are committed to private service, come highly skilled and with extensive experience. As an agency we believe passionately that people skills are paramount. This is why we take great care in sourcing and nurturing candidates with such skills. We also recognize the value of communication in different languages particularly for young families and specialise in multilingual staff, e.g. Russian speaking nannies and tutors.

Our Staff. Your Asset.

We offer private household and family office staff worldwide on a permanent or contract basis, live-in or live-out.

We recognize that each client has highly individual requirements. A House Manager may also need to cook and drive for one family but not another. With our multi-talented staff, who work to very high standards we find the very best solutions for your particular needs.
